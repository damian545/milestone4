My History

Before I started this course, all of the coding languages were alien to me. In my working background, I have no use for any of this type of thing. So I had to start from scratch and if I missed anything during my lesson, I had to do all of my investigating when I got home from my work. This didn't leave me much time to do my homework, but I managed to get by considering my circumstances. Some of the languages are straight forward, while others were quite difficult because I don't use them on a day to day basis. Click on "Languages I know" to see how far I have come, enjoy.
